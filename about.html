About us

At GreenWaveCoast, we're passionate advocates for sustainable living in the breathtaking coastal region of Clatsop County. Our mission is simple yet profound: to inspire and empower individuals, families, and communities to embrace environmentally-friendly practices that preserve our natural resources, protect our ecosystems, and promote a harmonious relationship between humans and nature. We believe that sustainability isn't just a buzzword; it's a way of life—one that benefits both present and future generations. By fostering a culture of conservation, innovation, and responsibility, we strive to create a vibrant and resilient community where people and the planet thrive together

Mission

At GreenWaveCoast, our mission is to empower individuals and communities in Clatsop County to embrace sustainable living practices that preserve and protect our natural environment for future generations. Through education, advocacy, and community engagement, we strive to foster a culture of environmental stewardship, social responsibility, and economic resilience. By working together, we can create a greener, healthier, and more sustainable future for all.

History

GreenWaveCoast was born out of a shared passion for the breathtaking beauty and ecological diversity of Clatsop County. Inspired by the pristine coastline, lush forests, and vibrant communities that call this region home, a group of like-minded individuals came together with a shared vision: to promote sustainability and environmental conservation in our beloved coastal community.Our journey began with a simple question: How can we protect and preserve the natural wonders of Clatsop County for future generations? Determined to find answers, we embarked on a mission to raise awareness, inspire action, and effect positive change.From humble beginnings as a grassroots movement, GreenWaveCoast has grown into a dynamic platform for environmental advocacy, education, and outreach. Through our ongoing efforts, we've mobilized individuals, businesses, and policymakers to embrace sustainable practices and prioritize the health and well-being of our planet.But our story is far from over. As we continue to navigate the challenges of the 21st century, we remain steadfast in our commitment to building a more sustainable future—one where people and nature thrive in harmony. Join us on this journey as we work together to create a greener, healthier, and more resilient Clatsop County for generations to come.

Our Approach

At GreenWaveCoast, we take a holistic approach to sustainability, addressing environmental, social, and economic considerations in tandem. We understand that sustainable living is not one-size-fits-all; it's about making conscious choices that align with our values and priorities.Through education, outreach, and action, we empower individuals and communities to adopt sustainable practices in their daily lives. Whether it's reducing waste, conserving energy, supporting local businesses, or advocating for policy change, we believe that every small step towards sustainability makes a meaningful difference.
